Welcome to konektilo OPC-UA Browser

The konektilo OPC-UA Browser is an ionic app which you can use with konektilo to explore the structure of you connected OPC-UA servers. konektilo opc-ua browser is an open source project. For more information please visit the project page.

First steps

1. Enter the URL of konektilo on the left side. Authentication options are optional.

2. Save the entered data by clicking "SAVE & CONNECT". The connection status to konektilo is also displayed on this page.

3. Explore your OPC-UA servers by clicking on "Browser" in the bottom menu.
